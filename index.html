How the extradition process works in Spain

Spain is one of the most cautious jurisdictions in Europe. No request is granted automatically — courts examine evidence, human-rights risks, and political context.

01

Detention

Interpol/EAW. Arrests may happen at the airport, hotel, bank, or during routine checks.

02

First hearing

The judge sets your position: consent or defence. This stage is crucial.

03

Substantive review

Interpol/EAW. Arrests may happen at the airport, hotel, bank, or during routine checks.

04

Appeal

We challenge the decision. If needed — escalate to the European Court of Human Rights.
